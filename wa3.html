NYC Street Style & Seasonal Trends

New York City style changes with the weather, but one thing remains: individuality reigns. In the summer, it’s all about layering light textures, creative accessorizing, and blending vintage with trendy.

Image via Pinterest – NYC inspiration

The Power of Basics

Every great outfit starts with strong basics. Think white tanks, black mini skirts, and denim — they're the framework for endless creative combos. Skims and Levi’s are favorites for their quality and versatility.

Skims | Levi's

The Art of Styling Thrifted Pieces

Thrifting is a sustainable, fun, and expressive way to curate a wardrobe. It also combats the negatives of fast fashion by keeping clothes in circulation and supporting small sellers.

One skirt styled multiple ways with a standout bag

Belts, Boots, and Micro Shorts: Trend Highlights

Statement belts over micro shorts or skirt waists without loops? An obsession. And boots? They make everything cooler, especially in contrast with bare legs.

Micro shorts + belts = moment

One Piece, Many Looks

Capsule and sustainability meet when one item — like a Miu Miu crochet bag — can anchor countless outfits. These pieces become your signature.

The iconic Miu Miu bag styled across outfits

Capsule Wardrobes & Taking Risks

Capsule wardrobes aren't boring — they're freeing. Think fewer pieces, more styling power. It’s all about finding your look and playing with it boldly.

Check out Emma Chamberlain's podcast episode on capsule wardrobes.

Shopping Smart: Depop, Poshmark, and The RealReal

These platforms make fashion accessible, circular, and community-driven. Depop and Poshmark are best for thrifting + selling, while The RealReal is luxury resale heaven.
